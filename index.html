Pelion is a project incubation and delivery platform focused on transferring mature European technologies to international markets.

The name comes from the Pelion region in Greece, historically associated with knowledge, learning, and the practical application of expertise. This reflects our approach today: connecting experience with execution, and planning with implementation.

Pelion works with industrial partners, public authorities, and institutional stakeholders on B2B and B2G projects, supporting initiatives across energy, infrastructure, and applied technologies.

All activities are carried out within established procurement frameworks and regulatory environments.

What we do

Pelion acts as an incubator and integrator of projects, bringing together the relevant stakeholders under a single, coordinated framework.

  • Project structuring and definition of scope
  • Technology sourcing from established European providers
  • Coordination between public authorities, industrial partners, and private investors
  • Support across procurement, contracting, and implementation phases
  • Follow-up through to project delivery and operational readiness

Pelion remains engaged throughout the project lifecycle, supporting continuity from initial concept to execution.

Technology and Markets

Pelion works with technologies that are already applied, regulated, and operational in Europe. These solutions are transferred and adapted to international markets where institutional or industrial demand exists, local partnerships are in place, and regulatory and procurement frameworks allow for structured implementation.

Our activities span B2B and B2G projects. India represents a key focus area, alongside engagement with other regions where comparable partnerships and project conditions apply.

Our approach


Pelion follows a clear, process-driven approach aligned with professional and institutional standards.

Project governance

  • Defined governance structures and responsibilities
  • Aligned procurement and compliance pathways
  • Consistent documentation and reporting practices

Ecosystem coordination

  • Alignment with legal, financial, and regulatory requirements
  • Coordination of legal, financial, and technical advisors where appropriate
  • Unified operational framework for delivery

People & experience

Pelion is supported by a network of professionals combining technical expertise across engineering and applied technologies, entrepreneurial experience in project development and delivery, and practical knowledge of financing, structuring, and execution.

This combination supports projects that are feasible and deliverable in real operating environments.

Why Pelion

  • Continuous involvement from early structuring through implementation
  • Clear coordination between technology, capital, and execution
  • Alignment with institutional processes and procurement standards
  • Focus on long-term partnerships rather than transactional engagement

Our objective is to support projects that are technically sound, financially viable, and institutionally aligned.
